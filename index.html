Healthcare beyond boundaries

Extending expert diagnostic care to remote areas

Get in touch

For doctors, general practitioners, consultants, surgeons, specialists, pathologists, nurses, technicians and health administrators, technology should be a way of making healthcare service simpler.

No need to juggle with different systems to provide diagnosis and care to patients.

In reality resource constraints, inadequate diagnostic services and unavailability of clinical information abound. Even with advanced equipment links between different equipment - or between equipment and records systems - are not uniformly accessible for diagnostic data.

Healthcare service is adversely affected.

Patients suffer.

We offer a technology solution to make your work simpler, easier with the required information accessible through one single device, one digital window.

We provide a pathway to unify medical diagnostics and healthcare data.

Record
Deploy handheld devices at point-of-care health centres to integrate and record diagnostic health data and register patient information.
Integrate
Integrate devices with automated diagnostic equipment for lab tests made rapidly available across the platform.
Analyse
Rapid onsite screening using networked platform - health data available accurately, instantly for timely diagnosis and referral.
